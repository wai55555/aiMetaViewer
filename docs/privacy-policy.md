# Privacy Policy for AI Meta Viewer

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

## Overview

AI Meta Viewer is a Chrome extension that analyzes AI-generated images to extract and display metadata (such as prompts and generation settings) directly in your browser.

## Data Collection and Processing

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

- **Local Processing Only**: All image analysis is performed entirely within your browser (locally on your device). No image data or metadata is ever sent to external servers.
- **No Analytics**: We do not use any analytics services or tracking tools.
- **No User Accounts**: This extension does not require user registration or authentication.

## Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

- **`activeTab`**: To access images on the web pages you visit and analyze their metadata.
- **`host_permissions` (all URLs)**: To detect and analyze images across all websites you visit.
- **`storage`**: To save your preferences (such as minimum pixel count threshold and excluded sites) locally on your device.

**These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the extension and do not involve data collection or transmission.**

## Third-Party Services

This extension does **not** use any third-party services, APIs, or external servers.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
